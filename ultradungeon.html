What Is ULTRADUNGEON?
CONTRIBUTORS 💜:  Nathan Young, Isaiah Dakin
ULTRADUNGEON is an indie roguelike that has been in development since early 2023. It's a passion project that my co-developer, Nathan Young, and I have put blood, sweat, and tears into since its inception. The game is a conglomeration of genres, including bullet hell, roguelike, dungeon crawler, extraction fighter, & RPG. As of now, ULTRADUNGEON has over two hundred unique hand-sprited items, four unique NPCs, functional inventory & shop UI, and much more, which will be discussed later on this page!

What Makes it Possible?
Support the Godot Engine 💜
ULTRADUNGEON was initially planned to be developed using the UNITY engine, however shortly after the project's inception we made the decision to switch to the GODOT engine. Today the project is built upon the GODOT 4.3 game engine.

In the past two years, I have grown to truly love the Godot engine & its features. I cannot recommend this open source game engine enough. If you or someone you know plans to make a game, please give Godot a try!
